A converter from map coordinates to screen coordinates. The maps coordinates are real values, whereas the screen coordinates are integer values (pixels). By default, the y axis is oriented from bottom to top for the map coordinate, the other way around for the screen coordinates. The top left pixel of the screen has coordinates (0, 0).
